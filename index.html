Privacy Policy

1. How We Access and Collect Your Health & Fitness Data

Types of Data Collected:

  • Exercise data
  • Health test data
  • Nutrition data

Methods of Collection:

  • User Input: You may manually enter health and fitness information.

Purpose of Data Collection:

  • To track and analyze your health progress
  • To personalize workout plans and health insights
  • To improve app features and user experience

2. How We Store and Retain Your Data

Data Storage:

  • Your health and fitness data is stored securely on our servers or directly on your device, depending on the data type.
  • We use industry-standard security measures, including encryption and access controls to protect your data.

Retention Period:

  • We retain your health data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide services.
  • Your data is stored for 7 years after your last activity or account deactivation.
  • You may request data deletion at any time.

3. How You Can Delete Your Data

Your Rights and Options:

  • You can delete your data directly within the app by navigating to Menu > Delete My Account.
  • If you would like to delete your data while keeping your account, please contact our support team.

Data Deletion Process:

  • Upon your request, we will delete your health data from our systems within 7 days.
  • Deletion will remove all stored health and fitness data associated with your account.

4. Additional Information

  • We do not sell or share your health data with third parties for marketing purposes.
  • You have the right to access, correct, or delete your data at any time, as permitted by law.
